# Cedarbrae Eye Doctors: Monthly Eye Care Landing Page

This page is a simple patient-awareness tool for Cedarbrae Eye Doctors.

## What It Does

- Shows 12 monthly eye-care sections (January to December).
- Auto-selects a month based on the current minute of the clock.
- Smooth-scrolls the visitor to that month on page load.
- Lets users manually jump to any month using a compact mobile dropdown.
- Displays a weekly guidance card with practical booking prompts.

## 5-Minute Month Mapping

The page maps each 5-minute interval to one month:

- 00-04: January
- 05-09: February
- 10-14: March
- 15-19: April
- 20-24: May
- 25-29: June
- 30-34: July
- 35-39: August
- 40-44: September
- 45-49: October
- 50-54: November
- 55-59: December

## Safety and Triage Intent

This tool is educational only.

- It does not diagnose or treat eye conditions.
- It highlights prevention, common seasonal concerns, and when to book with CED.
- It includes emergency guidance for urgent warning signs (for example sudden vision loss, severe pain, dark curtain/shadow, injury), directing people to nearest hospital emergency services or 911.
- It also references Ontario Health811 (call 811) for non-emergency triage support.
